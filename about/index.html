The Vision

We see a future where anyone can freely transact regardless of geography, class or other divides. We believe the freedom to transact is foundational to a more fair, open, and equitable society.

The Mission

The exchange of assets as a means of participation in the world economy provides access to resources and increases social status. At the core of a more fair and equitable world is free participation without barriers, especially without the need to be permissioned by third parties.

Liquality's mission is to create robust, interconnected networks for peers to easily find each other and directly exchange any asset without intermediation.
